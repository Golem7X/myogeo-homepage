What's inside

Everything you need to study soil.

Geonify brings core soil mechanics into one focused mobile app — clear lessons, real practice, and a quick reference always at hand.

📚

Study 8 chapters

From soil composition and classification to consolidation, shear strength, and slope stability — explained with visuals and short notes.

🎯

Practice & take exams

Multiple-choice and calculation questions. Timed exam mode with A–F grading and history tracking so you can see real progress.

🃏

Flashcards & formulas

Spaced-repetition flashcards for long-term recall, plus a searchable formula sheet you can keep in your pocket.

Our Mission

Bridging experience and technology.

MYO_Geo_Orgs exists to close the gap between senior geotechnical expertise and the next generation of engineers — preserving the rigor of real engineering while removing the friction.

👷

Engineering Roots

Built on real geotechnical practice — field experience, lab workflows, and established standards (ASTM, Eurocode 7, JGS).

🔗

The Bridge

Connecting proven engineering knowledge with modern technology — keeping the rigor, removing the friction.

⚡

Digital Innovation

Web-based tools, structured calculations, and automated outputs — giving young engineers immediate access to senior-level methodology.
